Counterpart Assignment

One of the most exciting features of this section of African Literature is our plan to establish counterparts for all participants in this class with students in Africa!

Many of these students will be reading the same books as we are and, of course, they will know a lot about Africa. The students are from a "francophone" country--the national language in Senegal is French. They have studied English for several years -- usually English is their third or even fourth language.

The email exchanges that we establish and the conversations on nicenet.org will be opportunities for the African students to gain perspectives of Americans on Africa and African literature, as well as practice English.

We may also be able to have distinguised professors Dr. Abdou Ngom and Dr. Gorgui Dieng join in the conversation

This project is clearly experimental and may evolve and change throughout the semester. More precise plans for expectations and grading will also emerge.
