Wedding Gifts

 

We will be delighted if you can attend our wedding and we believe your support and affection are your most important gifts to us.

If, however, you feel you would really like to give a gift in celebration of the wedding, because we already have the things we need for our household, one gift that would delight us would be a donation to one of our favorite causes. What a wonderful way to spread the joy and love of our marriage to the whole community!

  • Our friend Amy works for Big Brothers and Big Sisters of Kalamazoo and assures us they do a wonderful job.
  • Cindy's cousin, Rebecca, works for Habitat for Humanity in Tennessee.
  • We are both fans of the environment. Allen's brother Tom works for Orlo, a creative, thought provoking environmental organization. Or, how about the Kalamazoo Nature Center or the Wilderness Society?
  • Other groups we support include Unicef, Oxfam, Amnesty International, PBS, GLSN, Humane Society, American Cancer Society, and Hospice.

Or we would love a bottle of wine or a copy of your favorite CD.
