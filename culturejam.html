Culture Jam Project

Propose a climate change culture jam, image or word based.

Begin by describing, and providing, if possible, the existing cultural meme (image or text) that you wish to disrupt/reinterpret/play off of. How did you find the meme? How does the meme work? Why is the meme important? Who is it associated with? What messages does it send in its original form? What politics and/or stance on global warming and/or climate justice does it represent in its original form?

Describe and, if possible, provide, the image or text that you would introduce to "jam" the cultural meme. How does your jam work? (You might refer here to what you have learned about culture jamming from links to our class website or your own research.) What alternative messages/information does your jam present? How did you come up with the jam? How might your jam help people better understand climate change, its causes, implications, issues of fairness, etc.?

Describe how you might introduce your jam to others. How might you support it going viral? If it were to go viral, how might you, and others, follow up?

This assignment need not be long, perhaps only a couple of pages, but it should be thoughtful, and, hopefully, clever and potentially powerful!

Plan to share your culture jam during the time set for our final exam.
