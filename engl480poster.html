Final: Poster Presentation at the
MCTE Bright Ideas Conference

Spring semester 2005 we hope will present an astonishing opportunity for the final exam, with your classmates and students from other English 480 sections, presenting at a poster session at a state-wide teacher conference. This session has been proposed to the conference organizers and needs to pass their approval -- something we should know a few weeks into the semester. In the event the conference presentation is not possible, there will be a take home exam.

You would need to:

  • prepare a tri-fold poster about a topic in teaching social justice in a secondary literature curriculum

  • develop handouts to accompany the poster

  • attend the conference and professional present your work.

Your poster will need its own title, be attractively designed, and addressed to your target audience, secondary English teachers.
