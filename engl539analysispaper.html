Analysis Paper

Drawing on one of the Middle Eastern texts or films you have read or viewed, write a careful analysis of this work's treatment of a postcolonial theme such as orientalism, nationalism, language, mapping, hybridity, neo-colonialism, essentialism, mimicry, Third World feminism, dependency theory, Arab nationalism, Islamism, intifada, etc.

This paper should be at least 5 pages long.
