Stance on Climate Justice

This assignment involves everyone in class writing on a different issue of climate justice, taking a stance, explaining the issue and its history, and proposing appropriate steps or actions to be followed to address injustice. The collection of essays will be published as separate pages on a publically viewable website or wiki.

Select an issue of climate justice from the list we develop in class - ideally everyone will find an issue that they care about, with everyone addressing a different topic. After doing a lot of careful note taking, writing, and thinking, formulate your stance in a single sentence.

Follow the statement of your stance with a full explanation of the issue. Provide relevant information so that an uninformed reader has a clear understanding of the importance and complexity of the issue. Include history, counter arguments, and diverse perspectives as you make a strong case for the stance you have taken.

Write in your own words, and feel free to refer to other sources of ideas and information. If they are online provide a link.

Your stance should be at least the equivalent of 3 double spaced pages.
