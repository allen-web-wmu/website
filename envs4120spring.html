Climate Emergency: Spring Into Action 2024

This semester our class will become better informed and directly involved in climate action by attending and participating in campus and community events that are part of the WMU Climate Emergency: Spring into Action. Events can be found on the Spring into Action Calendar.

Students are expected to attend one event every two weeks, and can earn extra credit by attending more.

After attending / participating in an event students are expected to write up a report of at least 200 words and email that report to the professor and all of the students in the class. Students are expected to read other student's reports - thus all of us become better informed.
