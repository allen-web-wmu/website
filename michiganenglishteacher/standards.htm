What are Michigan's current standards?

Existing State of Michigan Language Arts Content Standards (PDF)

State of Michigan Grade Level Content Expectations K-8 (GLCEs) (PDF) were developed in response to No Child Left Behind mandates and will be used as the basis of grade level testing. All schools and districts must align their curriculum with these standards, and over a three year phase-in process tests of "core matrix items" will be developed and implemented to directly assess student learning of the GLCEs. Schools will receive reports based on student test scores so that they can change their teaching in the hope that students will score better on selected portions of the grade level tests.

On the State of Michigan Department of English website "Grade Level Content Expectations Question & Answers" it is specified that English Language Arts at the High School level will follow the Michigan Curriculum Frameworks.
