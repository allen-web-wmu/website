Project Requirements as of Fall 2001

These bare bones requirements are directly from Allen Carey-Webb's list, please include anything else you'd like.

 

They are as follows:

Home Page(a.k.a. "Index.html") linking:
Parent/ Guardian/ Family Page
Student Page
Personal Page
Colleagues Page
Teaching Philosophy Page

On Your Home Page Include:
An Image of Yourself
An E-mail Link to Yourself
A Date Last Revised
You May Place Your Teaching Philosophy on This Page
The Same Background on All of Your Other Pages

On Your Parent/ Guardian/ Family Page Include:
Welcome Statement
Links Helpful to Parents/ Guardians/ Family Sites
Link to Student Page, or to Pertinent Student Information (grades, syllabus, field trips, etc.)
An E-mail Link
A Date Last Revised

On Your Student Page Include:
Recommended Reading List (annotated, with at least 10 books)
Links to Student Appropriate Sites
An E-mail Link
A Date Last Revised
A Link to Your Really Cool Webquest
Possible Threaded Discussion/ Chat Links
Link to Teaching Philosophy

On Your Personal Page Include:
Images of Yourself
A Link to Your Teaching Philosophy
Links to Your Resume and Portfolio, or Other Professional Materials
A Bit of Pertinent Personal Information
An E-mail Link
A Date Last Revised

On Your Colleagues Page Include:
Links to Helpful Lesson Plan Sites
Links to Possible Threaded Discussion/ Chat Links
Links to Interesting Cross-Curriculum Sites
Lessons that You have Developed
Your Personal Teaching Philosophy
Your Webquest
A Link To Your Group Website
An E-mail Link
A Date Last Revised
