Email Key Words

There is a legend about King Christian X of Denmark that, when the Nazis invaded Denmark and ordered the Jews to wear yellow stars, he put one on himself and asked all loyal Danes to do the same, thus preventing the Germans from identifying Jews and rendering the order ineffective.

Recently Republican-based organizations in Michigan and Wisconsin have requested under the Freedom of Information act all of the emails of certain targeted professors across our state and Wisconsin that include specific key words.

In Michigan those key words are: "Scott Walker Wisconsin Madison Maddow" and in Wisconsin they are: "Republican Recall Collective Bargaining Rally Union."

The Nobel Prize laureate Paul Krugman has written an important essay about this titled "American Thought Police." They are not yet coming for the Jews, but the fascistic nature of this request is clear.

I now have placed those words as part of the signature on every email I send.
