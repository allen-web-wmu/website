Western 4 Water Project

This project is a remarkable collections of activitives and fund raising events developed by students in African Literature Fall 2010 at Western Michigan University.

Inspired by their reading of contemporary literature from and about Africa, particularly Monique and the Mango Rains and a skype conversation with the author, Kris Halloway, the class decided to address the clean water crisis in Africa.
